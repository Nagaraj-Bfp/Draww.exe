Rules of the Game

  • Multiple rounds; each round one player draws, others guess.
  • Draw only with pictures — no letters or numbers.
  • Guessers type guesses in the chat. Fast correct guess = more points.
  • Drawer earns points when someone guesses; highest total wins.
  • Don’t say or type the word if you’re the drawer!

How to Play

1

Wait for the host to start. One player will be chosen to draw.

2

Drawer gets a secret word — illustrate it using brush, colors & eraser.

3

Everyone else types guesses in chat. Blanks and hints appear over time.

4

Score points for correct guesses. After all rounds, the top score wins!
